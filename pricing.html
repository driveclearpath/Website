Everything you need to know about ClearPath and how we work.

ClearPath Holdings is a parent company that owns and operates multiple businesses across software, events, and service industries. We build operational intelligence platforms, run real events, and operate real businesses — all powered by our own technology. Our four product lines are ClearPath OS (shop management intelligence), ClearPath Experience (shop TV displays), ClearPath Events (event operations), and ClearPath Voice Intelligence (phone system & coaching). Everything we build, we use. Everything we use, we improve.
ClearPath OS is a Virtual COO for automotive repair shops. It connects to your shop management system, syncs every five minutes, and gives each person on your team exactly what they need: advisors get daily game plans and recovery lists, techs see their performance scoreboard, and owners get an AI-powered morning briefing with dollar-specific actions. It has 10 modules, 12 diagnostic engines, 65+ KPI guardrails, and 25+ revenue leak types it detects automatically.
ClearPath Experience is a TV display system for shops. Mini PCs mount behind TVs and show four different screen types: a lobby screen with live TV, news, stocks, and vehicle status for customers; a counter screen with staff bios and trust messaging; a floor screen with team ops data; and an owner office screen with the full business dashboard. Pair it with a QR code on first boot and it runs forever.
ClearPath Events is a complete event management platform with online registration, Stripe payments, sponsor & vendor management, volunteer scheduling, merchandise sales, and a mobile staff app with POS. Built for a 45,000+ attendee car show. See it live at cruisingdowntownnh.com.
Our AI receptionist answers your shop phone 24/7, handles appointment requests, routes calls, and manages emergencies. After every call, the system grades the conversation and flags missed booking opportunities. It feeds directly into ClearPath OS.
ClearPath OS is built for independent automotive repair shop owners and operators who want real-time visibility and control. ClearPath Experience is for any shop that wants ambient awareness on TVs. ClearPath Events serves event organizers who need a complete operations platform.
