Pricing questions

Asked before.
Answered straight.

01
Why "starting at" instead of a fixed price?

Because a two-bay independent and a five-location group shouldn't pay the same number, and pretending otherwise means one of them is overpaying. The tier prices are real floors, not bait — the working session is where we scope your actual number, and it's in writing before you commit to anything.

02
What happens in the $249 working session?

It's a real planning session with the founder, not a sales call. We map how your operation runs today, design which ClearPath pieces fit, walk your integration plan, and sketch your first 30 days. You leave with the plan either way — and if you sign, the $249 is credited to your first month.

03
Are there contracts or setup fees?

No long-term contracts — ClearPath earns its seat month to month. No setup fees on OS. Experience has a $99 one-time setup only if you take the pre-configured Firestick option.

04
What does my data get used for?

Running your business. Nothing else. We never sell customer data and we never train AI models on your operations — that's contract language, not a marketing line.

05
How do I get started?

Book the working session at info@driveclearpath.com, or spend five minutes with our AI intake first and we'll come to the session already knowing your operation. We're selective — fewer operators, better results.
