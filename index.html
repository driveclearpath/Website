About ClearPath

Built by an owner who couldn't be at the shop.

ClearPath started when life pulled its founder away from the family shop — a business his father started. The shop was still running, but without real-time visibility, decisions were made on emotion instead of data. Revenue leaked. Bottlenecks went unnoticed. Good people made bad calls because they didn't have the facts in front of them.

So he built a system that changed that. Real-time guardrails. Coaching that talks to your team when you can't. Data that replaces arguments with answers. ClearPath exists because one person refused to let distance stop him from growing what his family started.

ClearPath Holdings is a parent company that owns and operates multiple businesses across software, events, and service industries. We build operational intelligence platforms, run real events, and operate real businesses — all powered by our own technology. Our divisions include ClearPath OS (shop management intelligence), ClearPath Events (event operations), and more on the way. Everything we build, we use. Everything we use, we improve.
ClearPath OS is a Virtual COO for automotive repair shops — built for our own shop first. It connects directly to Tekmetric, watches your numbers in real time, and gives your employees coaching and tools to stop bottlenecks, plug revenue leaks, and maximize profits. 65+ KPIs, 12 diagnostic engines, and AI-powered game plans — the same operational intelligence that big chains pay entire departments to produce.
ClearPath Events exists because Cruising Downtown Manchester was running old school — paper lists, cash boxes, and Venmo requests. We started building a website, but one thing led to another and it became a full event management platform. It now handles registrations, payments, sponsorships, vendors, volunteers, merchandise, and day-of-show operations for 45,000+ attendees. Staff phones become tap-to-pay terminals. Every dollar is tracked by type, method, and staff member. See it live at cruisingdowntownnh.com.
ClearPath OS is built for independent automotive repair shop owners and operators who want data-driven insights. ClearPath Events serves event organizers who need a complete operations platform — from car shows to charity events.
