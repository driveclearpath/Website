ClearPath Practice doesn't replace your advisors. It removes the chaos around them — so the conversations that matter aren't buried in the ones that don't. The aggregators win because they have process. We give you theirs.

01

AI intake that knows your advisors.

Answers the line. Knows every advisor, their specialty, and who their clients are. Handles password resets, login help, and routine FAQs without escalating. Captures structured messages with reason and contact info when nobody is free to take a call. The phone stops being a black hole.

02

Advisor portal · the queue that doesn't lie.

Personal login for every advisor across every office. Live count of pending callbacks, oldest message age, and resolution status. Email and on-screen delivery so messages never get missed. The queue surfaces what's rotting — nothing sits past twenty-four hours without lighting up red.

03

Follow-up that triggers on what actually matters.

Tracks family context: spouses, kids, ages, milestones, dependents. Triggers outreach on the events that compound — kid turns 18, 401k window opens, insurance review comes due. Extracts action items from your meeting transcripts the moment the call ends. Your team stops forgetting what your clients told you last quarter.

04

Recovery pipeline for the silent leaks.

Silence is a signal. ClearPath tiers every quiet client into hot, warm, or cold based on overdue commitments, stale concerns, and time since last contact. Surfaces the ones you're bleeding before competitors find them, with one-click AI-drafted outreach in the advisor's voice. The practice stops losing households to firms that just answered the phone faster.

Advisor questions

The objections we hear.
Answered straight.

01
We already use Redtail / Wealthbox / Salesforce. What does ClearPath replace?

Nothing. ClearPath runs on your phone line, your email, and your calendar — outputs feed your CRM the same way meeting transcripts already do. No double-entry, no migration. Your CRM stays your system of record.

02
How does the AI handle FINRA / SEC compliance?

Call recording disclosure on every inbound. Retention rules consistent with your firm's policy. Supervised principal access for review. Audit trail on every AI-drafted outreach — nothing leaves the platform without a human review and send. We're built for the regulated industry we serve.

03
What does the AI receptionist do that a human can't?

It's there at 6 AM on a Saturday when your office isn't. It knows every advisor by name and specialty. It handles password resets and login help without escalating. And it costs less than the part-time receptionist you've been meaning to hire — without the turnover.

04
Will my advisors actually use it?

Adoption is the workflow. The advisor portal is the same screen they're already glancing at when the phone rings. The AI-drafted outreach lands in their email like everything else. There's no second tab, no separate app, no training course — just a queue that doesn't lie and a recovery pipeline that surfaces what's leaking.

05
What about my client data — does it train AI?

No. Client account data is never touched. Conversations are processed in real-time reasoning sessions — no retention by AI providers, no training, no aggregation, no resale. We sign that into the contract.
