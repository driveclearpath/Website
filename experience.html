How It Works

Set up once.
Runs forever.

1

Mount the Mini PC

A small PC mounts behind any TV with a standard VESA bracket. Plug in power, plug in HDMI. That's the hardware.

2

Scan the QR Code

On first boot, scan a QR code to pair the screen to your shop and select the screen type. Lobby, counter, floor, or office.

3

Walk Away

The screen auto-updates from Supabase. No maintenance, no logins, no interaction required. It just works.
