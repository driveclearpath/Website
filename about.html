About ClearPath Holdings

We build for the operator
who refuses to sell.

ClearPath is a family of operational software products for independent service businesses. Auto shops. Financial advisor practices. Event operators. The common thread: small operators competing with corporate roll-ups that have better software, better data, and a longer runway. We build the layer that closes that gap.

There's a reason small family businesses keep getting bought up by corporate America.

It isn't that they're bad businesses. It's that they don't have the operational sophistication to compete with the firms doing the buying. Caliber and Driven Brands have a playbook. Mariner and Creative Planning have a playbook. The managed-services firms quoting your event operations have a playbook. The roll-up wins because the family-owned operator can't see what the corporate playbook sees — the leaks, the patterns, the relationships quietly slipping.

We started ClearPath to flip that. To give the independent shop, the family practice, the local operator the same operational layer the corporations have. Not as a luxury. As a defense.

What we believe

Operational sophistication is no longer optional. The roll-ups have spent two decades building it. They've measured every variable. They've codified every decision. When they walk into your zip code looking for the next acquisition, they know exactly what good looks like — and they know which independents are operating below it.

The independent operator has been told the answer is "work harder," "care more," "treat the customer like family." Those are necessary. They're not sufficient. The roll-ups care less than you do, work fewer hours than you do, and still win — because they have software, data, and process discipline you don't.

ClearPath gives you the same software, the same data, the same process discipline. Pointed at keeping you independent.

What we build

Four products in the family. Each addresses an operational layer the corporate version of your business already has.

  • ClearPath OS — virtual COO for independent auto shops. The same KPIs the chains use to evaluate which shop to buy next, pointed at running yours.
  • ClearPath Practice — practice management for independent financial advisors. Every call, every callback, every life-event follow-up tracked, delegated, resolved.
  • ClearPath Experience — shop TV displays. Lobby, counter, floor, owner office. Each screen tuned to its audience.
  • ClearPath Events — operations platform for independent event operators. Registration, sponsors, vendor placement, volunteer coordination, security gate, all on one panel.

Same thesis applied to four industries. Different operational vocabulary. Same goal: arm the operator who refuses to sell.

How we work

Real-world use first, SaaS second. Every product in the family was built because a real operator needed it. ClearPath OS came out of running a shop. ClearPath Events came out of running an event. We don't build features for hypothetical customers. We build them for the operator we were yesterday.

Build once, use everywhere. The operational vocabulary differs across industries, but the patterns rhyme. Recovery pipelines, KPI diagnosis, ambient awareness, integration discipline — once we've built the layer in one industry, the next industry is faster and better. ClearPath is a portfolio because the underlying engine is the same.

Independent stays independent. We will never roll up our customers. We will never sell their data. We will never train AI models on their operations. Our customers are the ones who refused to sell their independence — we won't be the firm that erodes it through the back door.

Where we are

ClearPath is headquartered in Manchester, NH. We're a small team. We don't have outside investors and we're not raising. We grow at the speed of customer success rather than the speed of someone else's exit timeline.

We're actively pursuing pilots in all four industries. If you're an independent operator who wants what the roll-ups have without becoming one, talk to us.
