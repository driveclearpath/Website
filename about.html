About ClearPath

Built from the inside.
Not the outside looking in.

Every tool in ClearPath was forged from a real problem in a real business. That's why it works.

Small family businesses deserve to win.

Every day, independent shops lose money they don't even know is missing. Revenue leaks through missed follow-ups, bad estimates, slow bays, and decisions made on gut instead of data. The big chains don't have that problem — they have systems, dashboards, and corporate playbooks running every location like a machine.

ClearPath was built to close that gap. We give family-run businesses the same operational intelligence that corporate America uses — real-time KPIs, AI coaching, automated recovery, and visibility from anywhere. Not so you can become corporate. So you can't be beaten by them.
