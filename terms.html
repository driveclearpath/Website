Messages are sent from the shop's designated phone number and identify the shop by name. Customers provide consent to receive these messages when they provide their phone number during the vehicle intake process at the shop. Message frequency varies based on service activity (typically 1-4 messages per visit). Message and data rates may apply. Customers may opt out at any time by replying STOP. For help, reply HELP.
